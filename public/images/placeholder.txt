- logo.png - Site logo
- hero-bg.jpg - Hero section background image

- free-case.png - Free case image

## Case Images:

## Additional Assets:
You can add more images as needed for cases, items, and UI elements.

For demo purposes, you can use placeholder images from services like:
- https://picsum.photos/ for random images
- https://via.placeholder.com/ for solid color placeholders
- CS:GO community images for authentic case/weapon images
